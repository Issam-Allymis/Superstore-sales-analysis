# Superstore Customer & Regional Analysis
## Overview 
This project explores customer purchasing behaviour and regional performance using retail sales data. The goal is to identify key revenue drivers, profitability issue, and the impact of discounts on business performance.

---

## 🏹 Objective
To analyse how customers, regions, and product categories contribute to sales and profit, and to identify factors affecting profitability.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Kaggle Dataset
- Google Colab

---

## Dataset
The dataset used is the Superstore retail dataset containing information on: 
- Customer details
- Sales
- Profit
- Region
- Discounts
- Product categories

---

## Key Analysis

### 1. Top Customers By revenue
 - Identified customers generating the highest total sales
   **Finding:**
   Revenue is highly concentrated among a small number of customers, indicating reliance on key accounts.

---

### 2. Customer Profitability Analysis
  - Compared customer sales, profit and discount levels
   **Findings:**
     Some high revenue customers generate low or negative profit due to higher discounts.

---

### 3. Regional Performance Analysis
  - Analysed sales, profit and discount by region
    **Findings:**
    The West region performs strongest overall, while the Central region shows lower profitability despite reasonable sales.

---

### 4. Category-Level Root Cause Analysis
 - Investigated which categories drive regional performance
   **Findings:**
   Furniture in the Central region is the main driver of low profitability due to negative profit margins and acquring the higher average discount.

---

## Key Insights
- Revenue is concentrated among a small group of customers
- High revenue does not always equal high profit margins
- Discounts significantly impact profitability
- The Central region underperforms due to furniture losses
- The West region is the most efficient overall

## Business Risk Identified
- Dependence on a small number of high value customers
- Profit leakage due to excessive discounts
- Loss-making product category within a key region

---

## Recommendations
- Review discount strategy for high value customers
- Reduce excessive discounting in the furniture category
- Focus on improving profitability in the Central region
- Maintain strong performance in the West region

## Conclusion
This analysis highlights that profitability is not solely driven by revenue. Effective discount maangement and product level performance significantly influence overall business success.
